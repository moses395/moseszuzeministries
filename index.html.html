WELCOME MY BRETHREN IN CHRIST

This page will teach you three things you need to work on in order to have an awesome relationship with God. As an introduction to this topic please you need to reflect on your journey with Christ and consider those things you need to work on. As you continue reading please meditate in this principles and be blessed. THANK YOU!

1. Anger

Anger makes us lose focus and control over our actions. As a christian we need to be always aware of things that makes go angry. Jesus is the center of our life, therefore Jesus must be our example on how we react through our situations and temptation. Lets pray God give me the power to overcome every spirit on anger in my life in Jesus Name.

2. Lust

Lust is the completely opposite of love. Lust is selfish, pride, envious. A christian must watch out of this infection because it drains out the life of a person and render useless everything somone has worked for. Prayer: God set me free from the spirit of lust and give me the grace to follow you. Amen

Laziness

Every Christian need to have the desire to work hard. Being lazy is not bibical. Each and every day you need to pray, read the bible and find something to do to support finacially. Being lazy is not an excuse of being spiritual. you can be spiritual and working at the same time. Prayer: God help me to overcome laziness in my life. In Jesus Name. Amen

Thank you for you time remember to pray always and study the word of God. God bless you!
